I’m on the whole a rather agreeable fellow who’s trying to earn an honest living making people smile and dance. There are many words used to describe people of my profession: musician, artist and overall disappointment, just to name a few. Personally, I like to think of myself as someone who decorates time.

My songs are available on Spotify, Apple Music and a plethora of other streaming platforms. If you want to own the music you listen to, as it were, I urge you to take a look over at my Bandcamp page. Here you’ll also find albums of the kind you can touch (and subsequently lose or break) as well as the odd memorabilia.
